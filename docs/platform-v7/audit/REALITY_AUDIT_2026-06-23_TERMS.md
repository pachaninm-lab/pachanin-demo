# platform-v7 audit terms — 2026-06-23

## Terms

- `controlled-pilot`: safe limited contour; not live high-volume readiness.
- `pre-integration`: external providers are not connected as production dependencies.
- `audit draft`: record prepared for review; not final execution proof.
- `external confirmation`: bank, document, logistics, lab, elevator or field confirmation outside the current UI.
- `money at risk`: exposure or settlement risk shown for decision support; not a claim that money has moved.

## Use

Use these terms in follow-up role-cabinet PRs to keep status honest.
